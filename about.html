We are Luminary Studio.

Our Story

Built on craft,
driven by vision.

2018

Luminary was founded in 2018 by a small team of designers and strategists who believed that great design is not a luxury — it's a necessity. We started in a shared workspace with a handful of clients and a clear conviction: that every brand deserves to be seen, remembered, and loved.

"Design isn't decoration. It's the difference between being seen and being ignored."

Over seven years, we've grown from a scrappy three-person studio into a team of eighteen creative professionals spanning design, development, copywriting, and strategy. But what hasn't changed is our obsession with quality — every brief is treated as an opportunity to make something remarkable.

We work with clients across industries: from startups finding their voice for the first time, to established businesses ready for a transformation. What unites them is a shared belief that how you present yourself to the world matters enormously.

Today, Luminary operates from its studio in London, working with clients across Europe, North America, and beyond. Our work has been recognised by the Design Week Awards, the Webby Awards, and featured in publications including Print Magazine and It's Nice That.

What We Stand For

01

Craft Over Speed

We take the time to do things properly. We'd rather deliver one extraordinary thing than three adequate ones. Deadlines matter, but so does the work.

02

Honesty Always

We tell clients what they need to hear, not just what they want to hear. Genuine partnership means being straight about what will and won't work.

03

Curiosity First

We ask a lot of questions. About your business, your audience, your ambitions. The best creative work starts with deep understanding, not assumptions.

04

Lasting Impact

Trends fade. We design for longevity. Our goal is work that looks as relevant in five years as it does today — timeless without being predictable.

FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope, but a brand identity project typically takes 6–10 weeks. A full website might be 8–14 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline estimate in our initial proposal.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. Some of our most exciting work has been with early-stage startups building their brand from the ground up. We offer packages suited to different budgets.

What's your process like?

We start with a discovery phase to understand your business and goals. Then we move into strategy, creative development, refinement, and delivery — keeping you involved throughout.

Do you offer ongoing retainers?

Yes. Many of our clients work with us on an ongoing basis for design support, new campaigns, and website updates. Get in touch to discuss retainer options.
